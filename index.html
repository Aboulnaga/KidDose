KidDose

KidDose: The safest and easiest way to calculate medication dosages for children.

With KidDose, you can:

  • Quickly and accurately calculate the correct dosage for any medication.
  • Easily find information about medications and their side effects.
  • Create and save medication profiles for your children.
  • Track your children's medication history.
  • Receive medication reminders and alerts.

KidDose is the perfect tool for:

Parents who want to ensure their children are receiving the correct medication dosages. Caregivers who need to administer medication to children.

Healthcare professionals who want to quickly and accurately calculate medication dosages.

  • Safe: Based on the latest medical guidelines.
